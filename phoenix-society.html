Phoenix Society

The Phoenix Society honors individuals who make provisions in their estate plans or who establish life-income gifts to benefit the University of Chicago. Planned gifts strengthen and sustain the future of education and research at the University.

Benefits

As a member of the Phoenix Society, you will receive:

A special memento

Invitations to exclusive University events

Invitations to exclusive University events

Subscription to Building for the Future newsletter

An opportunity to share your personal experiences and inspire others

Recognition in an annual honor roll (unless anonymity is requested)

Personalized assistance from gift planning professionals

View the honor roll

Membership

Lifetime membership is extended to individuals who make one or more of the following commitments to the University:

  • Include the University of Chicago in their estate plans
  • Create a charitable life-income arrangement to benefit the University
  • Name the University as a beneficiary of retirement assets or life insurance
  • Donate a personal residence or a vacation home to the University and reserve a life estate

There is no minimum gift amount required for membership in the Phoenix Society. To join, please fill out our membership form.

Questions? Contact Brenda Lee Johnson at phoenixsociety@uchicago.edu or 773.702.4113.
